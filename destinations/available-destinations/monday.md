# Monday

## Getting Started

Monday.com is a cloud-based work operating system that enables teams to plan, manage, and track projects, workflows, and customer relationships in a centralized, customizable platform, helping organizations streamline collaboration, automate processes, and drive productivity across departments.

#### Getting Started

1. Once you are in Census, navigate to [Destinations](https://app.getcensus.com/destinations).
2. Click the New Destination button.
3. Select Monday in the dropdown list
4. Access your Monday Account and retrieve your [global API token](https://developer.monday.com/apps/docs/choosing-auth#method-3-using-a-users-global-api-token).
5. Copy your token into the API Token and click save.
